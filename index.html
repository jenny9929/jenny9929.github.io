About Jenny

Art found me when I
had something to say.

And I haven't stopped since.

Cross-Industry Independent Artist Bay Area NFC Pioneer

Grew up in China, educated across Switzerland, Germany, and the US, and built a career spanning law, finance, and tech. Along the way, I felt something most women know but rarely say out loud — the invisible weight of being expected to fit a mold that was never made for you. That feeling became the reason I created "The World Opens for Her": art as an act of encouragement, for every woman who has ever wanted to break free and see what the world holds for her.

Now based in the Bay Area, I couldn't help but ask: what if art and technology could speak the same language? That question became NFC ART. My pieces have since reached tech professionals and companies across the US. I was among the first in the Bay Area to create tech art gift sets, where hand-crafted gift sets tell a company's story through art.
