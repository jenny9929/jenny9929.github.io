About Jenny

Art found me when I
had something to say.

And I haven't stopped since.

Cross-Industry Independent Artist Bay Area NFC Pioneer

Law. Finance. Tech. Three industries that rarely make space for women to be fully themselves. I felt that. And I wanted to do something about it. Art became my answer.

Because I've lived inside these worlds, I see them differently — and I paint them that way. That's a perspective that's entirely my own.

Based in the Bay Area, I couldn't help but ask: what if art and technology could speak the same language? That question became NFC art. My pieces have since reached tech professionals and companies across the US — and for many of them, it was their first time experiencing technology and aesthetics in the same breath.
